Application that's Based on You.

Ticsee will connect you with the local authentic restaurant and attraction places in Taiwan. You can get vegetarian, halal food, and other authentic foods. Say good bye to "empty tummy". Wait, are you feeling bored? Open Ticsee app, get your backpack, put your shoes on, and get ready for the best attraction.

Cheaper Price.

Ticsee collaborate with many local Taiwanese restaurants and attraction places. Therefore we build trust to give an affordable price for international visitors to buy foods and attraction place ticket.

Smart Recommendation.

With the power of deep learning, Ticsee will give you the best recommendation based on your preferences and your last activity. Is it a magic? Nope, science is the answer.

Updated.

Our collaboration with local restaurants and attraction places gives us fully-updated and complete information about them. We hope, you will never see closed restaurant because of wrong information :).

How The App Works?

Search

Find delicious food based on your preferences. You can choose to search vegetarian, halal food, or other authentic food for your tummy needs.

Book

After you found the restaurant or the place you want to go, you can reserve your seat there with Ticsee.

Pay with Cheaper Price

Don't be shocked with the price. We collaborate with the local restaurants or event organizer of the place so you can get lower price than on site payment.

Enjoy

Feel the authentic foods such as Halal and Vegetarian for your daily meals. Don't forget to enjoy your trip to authentic places around using Ticsee apps.
